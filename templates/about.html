About Us

Jitterbug Coffee Hop has been a staple in Sugarhouse since Bruce Holt opened the doors January 24th, 2002. Bruce has been delighting customers with his espresso-based drinks, home-made sandwiches, nachos, shakes, and smoothies for over 13 years now. The atmosphere is that of a 1950's diner, but with modern taste in beverages and food selections.

Speaking of food, Bruce offers a scrumptious selection of community favorites, and if it's not on the menu, just ask and he'll make it just for you. There are some specialty drinks that are not on the menu: Ask about the Bug Latte and the Firebug (a spicier version of the Jitterbug Latte).
